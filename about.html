ABOUT ME

Alexis is a senior at the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill. In the School of Media and Journalism her concentration is multimedia. Her second major is media production in the communications department. She is also minoring in history.

Along with her busy class schedule, Alexis works part time at UNC New Media (or GoHeels TV) as a production assistant. She does videography and video editing for the Olympic sports on campus. She is very flexible in the work she does there. She has worked in some capacity on several different projects that was either produced online or shown at football and basketball games.

Alexis was a staff writer for the Daily Tar Heel during her sophomore year. She was a sports writer, covering sports such as women's lacrosse, volleyball, fencing and rowing. She was able to write a blog for the site about former UNC basketball player Ty Lawson and his return to Chapel Hill. Although Alexis loved covering sports, she also enjoyed a city story she was assigned her first summer writing.

Going forward, Alexis looks to combine sports, video and web design into her future endeavors. However, she also has a passion for music, social justice and education. So she looks to forward to combining her passions and her work to help others.

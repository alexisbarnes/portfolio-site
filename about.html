ABOUT ME

I am a senior at the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill. In the School of Media and Journalism my concentration is multimedia. My second major is media production in the communications department. I am also minoring in history.

Along with my busy class schedule, I work part time at UNC New Media (or GoHeels TV) as a production assistant. I do videography and video editing for the Olympic sports on campus. I am very flexible in the work I do there. I have worked in some capacity on several different projects that was either produced online or shown at football and basketball games.

I was a staff writer for the Daily Tar Heel during her sophomore year. I was a sports writer, covering sports such as women's lacrosse, volleyball, fencing and rowing. I was able to write a blog for the site about former UNC basketball player Ty Lawson and his return to Chapel Hill. Although I loved covering sports, I also enjoyed a city story I was assigned.

Going forward, I look to combine sports, video and web design into my future endeavors. However, I also have a passion for music, social justice and education. So I look to forward to combining my passions and my work to help others.
